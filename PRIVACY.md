# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** July 6, 2026
**App:** Ryft
**Developer:** WAYLM INC ("we", "us")

## The short version

Ryft is a local-first journal. **We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share
your personal data.** Everything you write, track, and photograph stays on your
device. There are no accounts, no ads, no analytics, and no tracking.

## Information we collect

**None that leaves your device.** Ryft has no servers that store your content.
The journal entries, checklists, notes, metrics, focus areas, photos, and
reminders you create are saved **only in local storage on your device** (an
on-device database and the app's private file storage).

We do not collect names, email addresses, location, contacts, advertising
identifiers, or usage analytics.

## Permissions we request

Ryft only asks for permissions needed for features you choose to use:

- **Camera / Photos** — so you can attach a photo to a journal entry. Photos you
  add are stored on your device and are **never uploaded** anywhere.
- **Notifications** — so the reminders you schedule can appear. Reminders are
  generated and delivered **locally on your device**; no reminder text is sent
  to us or anyone else.

You can revoke these permissions at any time in your device settings.

## Third-party services

Ryft does not include advertising or analytics SDKs.

If we ship an app update, your device may download it from **Expo Application
Services (EAS)**, the build/update infrastructure Ryft is built with. This
update check may involve standard technical information (such as your IP address
and app/OS version) being processed by Expo to deliver the correct update. **No
journal content or personal data is ever included.** See Expo's privacy policy
at https://expo.dev/privacy.

## How your data is stored and secured

Your data lives on your device and is protected by your device's own security
(lock screen, OS sandboxing). Because we never receive your data, we cannot
access, view, or recover it.

## Data retention and deletion

You are in control. Deleting an entry removes it from your device. **Uninstalling
Ryft permanently deletes all of its data from your device.** Since we hold none
of your data, there is nothing for you to request us to delete.

## Children's privacy

Ryft is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any
information from anyone, including children.

## Open source

Ryft is open source. You can review exactly what the app does at
https://github.com/Waylm/ryft.
